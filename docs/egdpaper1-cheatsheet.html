EGD Paper 1 — Cheat Sheet

Samson Chrizeecry Senyatsi — Technical Mastery Academy

1 — Exam Mindset & Timing

Paper 1 structure: technical drawing problems. Aim for clarity. Manage time by allocating equal slots per question; start with easiest.

2 — Tools & Setup

  • HB / 2H pencils, mechanical 0.5mm, scale rule, protractor, compass, set squares.
  • Light construction lines, bold final outlines.

3 — Common Drawing Steps

  1. Read fully, annotate constraints.
  2. Set datum/baseline and block main shapes.
  3. Add construction lines, then finalize line weights.

4 — Key Geometry Tips

Tangency: draw radii to tangent point. For bisectors use compass arcs. Use similar triangles for proportions.

5 — Scale & Measurements

Always write the scale. Use scale rule; double-check conversions.

6 — Presentation & Mistakes

Title block, clear dimensions, avoid dark construction lines, never skip scale annotation.

7 — Quick Formulas

  • Pythagoras: a² + b² = c²
  • Trig: sinθ, cosθ, tanθ
  • Circle: C = 2πr, A = πr²

8 — PAT Tips

Practice timed past papers; keep templates for recurring question types; follow a problem checklist.

9 — Short Daily Routine (30 min)

10 min review, 15 min redraw, 5 min self-check.
